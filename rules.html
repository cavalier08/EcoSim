How to play

  • Pick plants and animals to form your ecosystem!
  • When you're ready, click on the 'submit' button, and watch the simulation take place!
  • See how long your ecosystem lasts before an integral part of the biodiversity is lost.
  • Take note of what you see.
    • What does a healthy ecosystem look like?
    • Which animals are the most susceptible? Why might that be?
    • How does an animal's starting population affect the ecosystem?
    • Are there any strategies to producing a healthy, balanced ecosystem?
  • At the end of the simulation, you'll have the option of taking a small quiz to test your knowledge on plants, animals, and their relationship with each other!
  • When you're ready to play, hit the "play" button at the top of the screen! Good luck!
